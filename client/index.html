Smart Traffic Accident Risk Prediction System

A data-driven platform that analyzes traffic conditions and predicts accident risk levels to help improve road safety and traffic management.

Overview

Rapid urbanization and increasing traffic density have created new challenges for transportation systems. Understanding how environmental and traffic conditions affect accident risk can help authorities take preventive measures.

This system predicts accident risk levels using traffic parameters such as weather, time of day, road type, and traffic density.

Why This System Matters

Traffic accidents cause significant loss of life and economic damage every year. Predicting accident risk can help traffic planners and drivers become more aware of dangerous conditions.

By analyzing traffic factors and presenting the results through maps and analytics charts, this system provides meaningful insights into road safety.
